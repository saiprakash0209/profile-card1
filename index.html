General Anil Chauhan

Awards

  • Vishisht Seva Medal in 2011
  • the Sena Medal in 2014
  • the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal in 2015
  • the Uttam Yudh Seva Medal in 2018
  • the Param Vishisht Seva Medal in 2020

Anil Chauhan was born in a Hindu Garhwali Rajput family of Chauhan clan on 18 May 1961, hailing from the Pauri Garhwal district, Uttarakhand. After completing his schooling at Kendriya Vidyalaya at Fort William, Kolkata, he joined the National Defence Academy, Khadakwasla (NDA) as part of the 58 Course in year.
